Advancing the lung health of firefighters

Firefighters face repeated exposure to hazardous smoke and airborne carcinogens during structural fires and wildfires. Long-term respiratory effects deserve visibility, resources, and coordinated support.

Through evidence-based research, policy advocacy, direct care initiatives, and our growing network of youth-led chapters, we're creating measurable change in firefighter respiratory health.
