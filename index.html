How we create impact

Our model is designed to be measurable and repeatable—so chapters can lead real change locally.

  • Hydration & recovery drives for fire stations
  • “Letters for Lungs” gratitude + advocacy campaign
  • Awareness posts and chapter spotlights
  • Collaborations with aligned nonprofits

Why we can't stay silent

Firefighters face repeated exposure to hazardous smoke and airborne particulates during structural fires and wildfires. Long-term respiratory effects deserve visibility, resources, and coordinated support.

Driven by purpose and impact

We support firefighter lung health by mobilizing communities, elevating awareness, and funding tangible station-focused resources.

Funding and advocacy nonprofit

We focus on fundraising and advocacy actions that chapters can run—backed by clear impact reporting and research-informed messaging.
