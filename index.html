Our Four Pillars

Everything we do is grounded in research, driven by youth-led action, and focused on measurable impact.

  • Research - Evidence-based approach to pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Advocacy - Policy and awareness initiatives for firefighter health
  • Care - Direct support and wellness programs for fire stations
  • Community - Youth-led chapters spearheading local initiatives

Advancing the lung health of firefighters, one breath at a time.

Firefighters face repeated exposure to hazardous smoke and airborne carcinogens during structural fires and wildfires. Long-term respiratory effects deserve visibility, resources, and coordinated support through research, advocacy, and care.

Research & Advocacy

Evidence-based approach to pulmonary rehabilitation. We partner with medical professionals and the American Lung Association to establish best practices for firefighter lung health.

Community Service & Care

Youth-led chapters spearhead initiatives including hydration drives, wellness programs, and direct support for firefighters in their communities.
