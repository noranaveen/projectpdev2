About Project Pulmonary

Driven by purpose and impact.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization centered around research, community service, advocacy, and youth-led impact. Our mission is to provide pulmonary rehabilitation to firefighters facing chronic exposure to airborne carcinogens.

501(c)(3) Nonprofit Youth-Led Organization Partnered with American Lung Association

Our mission

Your support changes the trajectory of firefighter lives worldwide. We provide pulmonary rehabilitation resources, fund practical initiatives, amplify awareness, and build a chapter network designed for measurable, sustainable impact.

What we do

We run hydration & recovery drives, “Letters for Lungs,” awareness campaigns, chapter spotlights, and collaborations with other nonprofits.

How we measure impact

We track chapter growth, number of drives, and the number of fire stations reached—so donors and sponsors can see outcomes.

What we believe

Health equity, community action, and research-informed advocacy can coexist—and scale.

Research

We spotlight credible research to keep messaging accurate and actionable.

Advocacy

We elevate firefighter respiratory risk and the environmental justice lens.

Care

We support stations and communities with practical resources and attention.
